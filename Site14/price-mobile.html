Price

The price for a custom design starts from 85 euro. The price does not include design correction. The cost of one correction is 15 euros (no more than 30% of the design). Payment is made after a personal consultation by email in full before the very process of creating a design.

After the payment I will create one design and send you the screenshot in the next two days. After that you check the design and if you have to change some elements, I can make it but it has separately price. After I will send you files in next formats: ai, pdf, jpg.

If you have any more questions, please see the answers to them in the section FAQ.
